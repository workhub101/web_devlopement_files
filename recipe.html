Recipe

Spaghetti Carbonara

    Ingredients:
  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 8 ounces pancetta or bacon,diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
    Cooking Instructions:
  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.Add spaghetti and cook until al dente, according to package instructions.
  2. In a large skillet,cook pancetta or bacon until crisp. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. In a small bowl,beat eggs,Parmesan cheese, and black pepper until well combined.
  4. Drain spaghetti and return to pot.Add pancetta or bacon,garlic, and egg mixture and stir until well combined.
  5. Cook over low heat for 1-2 minutes,stirring constantly,until sauce thickens and coats spaghetti.
  6. Season with salt and addtional black pepper to taste, and serve immediatly!
