Albert Einstein

Biography
"Imagination is more important than knowledge.For knowledge is limited, whereas imagination embraces the entire world,simulating progress,giving birth to evolution"

-Albert Einstein

Albert einstein was a German-born physicist who is widely considered to be one of the most influntial scientist of the 20th century.He is best known for developing the theory of relativity,one of the two pillars of mordern physics(alongside quantum mechanics).

Born in Ulm,Germany in 1879,Einstein moved to Switzerland as a child and later attended Swiss Fedral Insitute of Technology in Zurich.After graduating he worked as a patent clerk in Bern,Switzerland where he began to develop his ideas about relativity and the nature of space and time.

Einstein's groundbreaking work in physics earned him numerous awards and accolades throughout his lifetime,including the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1921. He also became known as a peace activist and advocate for nuclear disarmament
